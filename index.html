About Albi

Born in October 2012, Albi is a male maltese puppy living in Croatia

Most of the time cranky little being, but sometimes fluffy & cuddly. His favourite food is Mommy's risoto.

Likes long walks in the park, especially when leaves fall.

He is just chillin' at home

At the moment he is stationed in a small Croatian town called Donji Miholjac, missing his Mom who is on college in Osijek.
